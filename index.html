Who We Are

A clinic built on
trust, not transactions.

Phnom Penh Medical Centre was founded in 2009 by a Cambodian physician who spent a decade working in international hospitals and returned home with a clear vision: to make clinic-quality, evidence-based healthcare accessible to every resident of the capital — regardless of language, nationality, or insurance status.

Fifteen years on, we remain independent, family-owned, and deeply committed to continuity of care. You'll see the same doctor at every visit. You'll be explained every test we order. And you'll never be rushed out the door.

  • Registered with Cambodia Ministry of Health
  • All doctors hold internationally recognised qualifications
  • Electronic health records — your history, always accessible
  • Private consultation rooms, strict patient confidentiality
  • On-call emergency support for registered patients
